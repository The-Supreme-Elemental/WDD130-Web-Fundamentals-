Overview

Purpose

Our mission here at Oars of White is to provide the best whitewater
experience out there. With our state of the art rafts and our experienced
guides, this is sure to prove an unforgettable experience for the whole family!
We are excited for your visit!



Due to the violent nature of the river and inexperience of our guides, we would
recommend that only individuals between the ages of 20 and 40 shop here. WE DO ID YOU.
All other individuals not of these ages will be fined and prosecuted to the highest
extent of the law possible. Fines can be expected up to $500. Babies will be yeeted.

Audience

If you are looking to explore the wild and untamed waters for a great price, then we
are your people. Our guides each have at least 10 years of experience guiding the brave souls
through the magnificent rivers of the Horizon.
